# How to Grant Uprise Digital Access to Your Google Ads Account

To let us create, monitor, and optimize your Google Ads campaigns, please follow these two steps:

### 1. Retrieve your 10-Digit Google Ads Account ID
- Log in to your [Google Ads Dashboard](https://ads.google.com).
- Sign in if prompted.
- In the top right corner of your Google Ads interface, look for your **10-Digit Customer ID** (formatted like `XXX-XXX-XXXX` next to your email address).

<GoogleAdsIdForm />

### 2. Approve the Access Request
- Once you submit your ID above, our backend will trigger an connection request.
- Within 2 minutes, go to **Admin > Access and security > Managers** inside your Google Ads account.
- Look for the request from **Uprise Digital (Manager ID: 874-625-2766)** and click **Accept**.
