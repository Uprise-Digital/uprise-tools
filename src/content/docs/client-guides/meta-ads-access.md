# How to Grant Uprise Digital Access to Your Meta Ads Account

To let us manage and run your Meta (Facebook and Instagram) Ads, please follow these steps to add Uprise Digital as a Business Partner.

---

## Part 1: Granting Uprise Digital Partner Access

### Step 1: Navigate to Business Settings
1. Go to [Meta Business Settings](https://business.facebook.com/settings) and log into your business account.

![Meta Business Settings Home](/images/docs/facebook-ads/screenshot_1_1.png)

### Step 2: Open Partners Panel
1. In the left-side navigation menu, select **Partners** (under the **Users** section).
2. Under **Partner to share assets with**, click the blue **Add** button.

![Access Partners Tab](/images/docs/facebook-ads/screenshot_1_2.png)

### Step 3: Enter Uprise Digital's Partner ID
1. Provide the Partner Business ID for Uprise Digital: **`2448649278688629`** (you can click the button below to copy it directly).
2. Click **Next** to proceed.

<CopyMetaIdButton />

![Add Partner ID Dialog](/images/docs/facebook-ads/screenshot_2_3.png)

### Step 4: Share Your Facebook Page Access
1. Under the **Select asset type** panel on the left, click **Pages**.
2. Select the specific page(s) you want to share.
3. Scroll down under the rights list to find **Partial access (business tools and Facebook)**, and toggle **Content, Messages, Community activity, Ads, Insights** to **On**.

![Assign Page Permissions](/images/docs/facebook-ads/screenshot_2_4.png)

### Step 5: Share Your Ad Account Access
1. Under **Select asset type**, click **Ad accounts**.
2. Select your specific advertising account.
3. Under **Full control**, toggle **Manage ad accounts** to **On**.
4. Confirm your choices by clicking **Save changes**.

![Assign Ad Account Permissions](/images/docs/facebook-ads/screenshot_3_6.png)

That's it! You have successfully given Uprise Digital access to create and manage your Meta ads.

---

## Part 2: How to Create Your Ad Account (If You Don't Have One)

If you do not have a Meta Ad Account set up yet, follow these steps to create one:

### Step 1: Open Accounts Menu
1. Return to the [Meta Business Settings](https://business.facebook.com/settings).
2. In the left-side menu, click on **Ad accounts** (under the **Accounts** section).
3. Click the blue **Add** dropdown button and select **Create a new ad account**.

![Create New Ad Account Dropdown](/images/docs/facebook-ads/screenshot_4_7.png)

### Step 2: Configure Account Details
1. Input your advertising account details:
   * **Ad account name** (e.g. `[Your Brand Name] Ad Account`)
   * **Time zone**
   * **Currency** (USD, AUD, etc.)
2. Click **Next** and follow the prompts to save your new ad account.

![Ad Account Configuration Modal](/images/docs/facebook-ads/screenshot_4_8.png)
