# How to Grant Uprise Digital Access to Your Meta Ads Account

To let us manage and run your Meta/Facebook Ads, please follow these steps to add us as an official Business Partner:

### 1. Copy our Business Manager ID
Click the button below to copy the official Uprise Digital Business Partner ID to your clipboard:

<CopyMetaIdButton />

### 2. Add Uprise as a Partner in Meta Business Manager
- Log in to your [Meta Business Settings](https://business.facebook.com/settings).
- In the left sidebar, click on **Partners** (under the *Users* section).
- Click the blue **Add** dropdown button and select **Give a partner access to your assets**.
- Paste the copied Business Partner ID in the input box and click **Next**.

### 3. Assign Asset Permissions
On the next screen, toggle the assets and assign permissions:
- **Facebook Page**: Select your page and toggle **Manage Page** (or *Create Ads*).
- **Ad Account**: Select your ad account and toggle **Manage Ad Account** (Full Control).
- **Pixel / Datasets**: Under *Pixels* or *Datasets*, select your pixel and toggle **Manage Dataset**.
- Click **Save Changes** to finish.
